# One Piece Card Game - Official Rules Summary

## Game Overview

The One Piece Card Game is a competitive two-player card game where players assume the role of captains from the One Piece universe. The objective is to reduce your opponent's Life to 0.

## Deck Construction Rules

### Basic Structure
- **1 Leader card**: Placed in the Leader Area at game start. Does not count toward the 50-card deck limit.
- **50 cards**: Main deck consisting of Character, Event, and Stage cards.
- **Maximum 4 copies** of any card with the same card number in your deck (unless a card effect states otherwise).

### Color Restriction
- All cards in your deck must match at least one color of your Leader card.
- If your Leader is Red, you can only include Red cards.
- If your Leader is Red/Blue, you can include Red cards, Blue cards, or Red/Blue cards.

### Card Types
- **Leader**: Represents your captain. Has Life value and an activated ability.
- **Character**: Main units for attacking and defending. Have cost, power, and counter values.
- **Event**: One-time effect cards that are trashed after resolution.
- **Stage**: Persistent field cards that provide ongoing effects. Only one Stage can be active at a time.

## Game Setup

1. Each player places their Leader in the Leader Area.
2. Shuffle decks and draw 5 cards as starting hand.
3. Decide who goes first (Player 1) and who goes second (Player 2).
4. Player 1 starts with 0 Don cards. Player 2 starts with 1 Don card in their Don Area.

## Turn Structure

### Start Phase
- Draw 1 card from your deck.
- If your deck has no cards, you lose the game.

### Main Phase
- Play Character cards from your hand by paying their cost using Don cards.
- Play Event cards from your hand and resolve their effects.
- Play Stage cards from your hand.
- Attach Don cards to your Characters or Leader to increase their power.
- Activate abilities on your Leader, Characters, or Stage.

### Counter Phase (during opponent's attack)
- Play Counter cards from your hand to add power to your defending Character.
- Counter cards are trashed after the attack resolves.

### Attack Phase
- Declare an attack with one of your rested Characters.
- The opponent may counter with Counter cards or Character effects.
- If the attack hits the opponent's Leader, reduce their Life by 1.
- If the attack hits a Character, compare power. The lower power Character is trashed (K.O.'d).

### End Phase
- End your turn. The opponent's turn begins.

## Life Mechanics

- When your Leader is attacked, reveal the top card of your deck.
- If the revealed card has a **Trigger** icon, activate its effect before placing it in your hand or trash.
- If the revealed card has no Trigger, place it in your hand.
- When your Life reaches 0, you lose the game.

## Keywords

### Blocker
- Characters with Blocker can intercept attacks targeting your Leader.
- Must be rested to use Blocker.
- Redirect the attack to the Blocker character.

### Rush
- Characters with Rush can attack the turn they are played.
- Characters without Rush cannot attack the turn they enter play (summoning sickness).

### Banish
- Cards that are Banished are removed from the game entirely.
- Banished cards cannot be retrieved from the trash or deck.

### Double Attack
- Characters with Double Attack deal 2 damage to Life if the attack hits the Leader.

### Shield
- Characters with Shield cannot be trashed by opponent's card effects.

## Mulligan Rules

- After drawing your starting hand, you may declare a mulligan once.
- Shuffle your entire hand back into your deck.
- Draw a new hand of the same size.
- Player 2 draws 1 extra card after mulligan.

## Winning and Losing

- **Win**: Reduce opponent's Life to 0.
- **Win**: Opponent cannot draw a card when required (deck out).
- **Lose**: Your Life reaches 0.
- **Lose**: You cannot draw a card when required.
- **Concede**: A player may concede at any time, immediately losing the game.

## Don System

- Don cards are the resource system of the game.
- Each turn, you may put 1 Don from your deck into your Don Area (rested).
- Don in your Don Area can be attached to Characters or Leaders to pay costs or increase power.
- Each Don attached adds +1000 power to the Character/Leader.
- Don cards are returned to the Don Area when the attached card leaves play.

## Trigger Icons

The following Trigger effects can activate when revealed from Life:

- **Draw**: Draw 1 card from your deck.
- **Draw 2**: Draw 2 cards from your deck.
- **Counter**: Add 1 card from your trash to your hand that has a Counter value.
- **Rest**: Rest up to 1 of your opponent's Characters with a cost of X or less.
- **Fatal**: The attacking Character deals +1 damage (Double Attack for this attack).
- **Blank**: No effect. Place the card in your hand.

## Stage Rules

- Only 1 Stage can be on your field at a time.
- Playing a new Stage trashes your current Stage.
- Stage effects are continuous while the Stage is on the field.
- Stages have their own cost and are played during the Main Phase.

## Special Rules

- Cards that say "you may have any number of this card in your deck" ignore the 4-copy limit.
- Cards played from trash follow the same rules as cards played from hand unless stated otherwise.
- If multiple effects trigger simultaneously, the turn player chooses the order of resolution.
- "Trash" means the discard pile. Cards in trash can be referenced by effects.
